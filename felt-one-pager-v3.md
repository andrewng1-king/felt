## The Problem

Leaders want to know what their people really think. Employees want to tell them. But they can't — because the power dynamic makes it unsafe.

So the manager walks out of the conversation guessing. The employee walks out holding back. And slowly, quietly, the trust erodes.

- HR tools measure the damage after someone quits. Nobody prevents the conversation that caused it.
- Human coaching costs $300–500/month, is slow, and doesn't scale to every hard 1:1.
- No tool gives a manager the feedback their employee couldn't safely give them.

**The core pain:** Your best people are forming opinions about you after every conversation. You have no way to know what those opinions are — until they hand in their resignation.

**Why employees can't tell you:** They want to. But they can't afford to. You're their manager. They need their job. So they stay quiet, disengage slowly, and eventually leave. And you never knew what conversation started it.

## Solution

**felt.** reads the signals in your most important internal conversations — voice tone, language patterns, and transcript — and tells you what you couldn't see in the room.

**Pre-conversation**
- Role-play the hard talk with AI before it happens
- Stress-test your phrasing, detect emotionally loaded language
- Pre-Talk Readiness score: are you actually ready for this conversation?

**Post-conversation**
- Empathy Mirror: how the other person likely felt
- Full Conversation Intelligence breakdown across 11 metrics
- The specific moment things shifted — and exactly what you said that caused it

**Longitudinal**
- Behavioral Trends: your blind spots, tracked over time
- Risk Signals: is trust eroding in this relationship?
- Relationship memory: how this specific person is changing across conversations

## What Makes felt. Different

**The Empathy Mirror is the only metric in the market that analyzes the other person — not the manager.** Every other tool (Kona, 15Five, Gong) tells you how *you* performed. felt. tells you how *they felt*. That's a different product. That's a different category.

**Multimodal analysis.** felt. reads voice tone + speech patterns + transcript together. Not just what was said — how it was said, and what it triggered. This is what makes the inference accurate, not just suggestive.

**Built for internal conversations only.** Not sales calls (that's Gong). Not team standups. The specific, high-stakes conversations between a manager and someone who matters to them.

## Top 3 Risks & Mitigations

**1. Habit formation**
- Risk: Managers open it 3 times and stop
- Mitigation: felt. is triggered by important conversations, not all meetings — natural use case, not forced habit. First session delivers undeniable insight in under 5 minutes.

**2. Audio / Legal consent** 🔴 UNRESOLVED
- Risk: Voice analysis requires audio access. Many US states require two-party consent. GDPR in EU applies to biometric/voice data.
- Options: Via Fathom/Fireflies API (cleanest), self-record with in-app consent, built-in felt. recorder with explicit consent flow
- Must be resolved before go-to-market

**3. Microsoft / Kona closes the gap**
- Risk: Kona (15Five) already in the market. Microsoft Copilot could add an EQ layer.
- Mitigation: Go deeper on empathy mirror and multimodal analysis than either will care to. felt. builds for the individual. They build for scale. Be too personal to ignore, too niche to prioritize.
